Registration Process

Shortlisting of Teams

HackThisNovember

A shortlisting process would be done from which certain teams would be selected out of the lot to take part in HackThisNovember. Shortlisting process begins from 13th November and ends on 15th November 2022

Nov 18 - Nov 19 Hackathon

Hackathon Begins for all

HackThisNovember

The Hackathon would begin on 18th November, 2022 at 11AM and end on 19th November, 2022 at 11AM. It is a 24hr hackathon where you'd be given various problem statements from various domains for your team to choose from along with mentors guiding you in certain time gaps.

Nov 19, 11AM Judging
